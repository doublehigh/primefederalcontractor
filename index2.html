About the Company

A Veteran-Led Firm Built for Government

Prime Federal Contractors LLC is a certified government contracting company headquartered in Commerce City, Colorado. We specialize in IT solutions, cybersecurity, professional services, and facility support for federal, state, and local government agencies.

Founded by a U.S. military veteran with over 7 years of hands-on experience in cybersecurity, infrastructure security, and regulatory compliance, our firm brings the precision and discipline of military service to every contract we execute.

IT & Cybersecurity Solutions

Our cybersecurity practice delivers end-to-end information security solutions built for the federal environment. With deep expertise in DoD and civilian agency frameworks, we help organizations achieve and maintain compliance while strengthening their security posture.

  • Risk Management Framework (RMF) and Assessment & Authorization (A&A)
  • NIST SP 800-53 / 800-171 control implementation
  • FISMA compliance and continuous monitoring
  • Zero Trust Architecture design and deployment
  • Penetration testing and vulnerability assessments
  • FedRAMP authorization support and cloud security
  • SIEM deployment, log management, and threat detection
